SPSP 2023: Historical Psychology Preconference

Mohammad Atari and Joseph Henrich have organized the first Historical Psychology preconference, which will occur during the Society for Social Psychology annual conference in 2023. This preconference will bring together researchers to discuss psychological variation over historical time. Confirmed speakers include James Pennebaker (University of Texas at Austin), Ted Slingerland (University of British Columbia), Tessa Charlesworth (Harvard), and Joshua Conrad Jackson (Northwestern University).
