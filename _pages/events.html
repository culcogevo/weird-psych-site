SPSP 2023: Historical Psychology Preconference

Mohammad Atari and Joseph Henrich have organized the first Historical Psychology preconference, which occured during the Society for Social Psychology annual conference in 2023. This preconference brought together researchers to discuss psychological variation over historical time. Speakers included James Pennebaker (University of Texas at Austin), Ted Slingerland (University of British Columbia), Tessa Charlesworth (Harvard University), and Joshua Conrad Jackson (Northwestern University).
